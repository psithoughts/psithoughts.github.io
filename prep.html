Public Sector Innovation Thoughts Hub

A Hackathon is festival of ideating, designing and coding by like-minded people who come together to address and build solutions to existing problems using digital or technological tools.

The Public Sector Innovation ThoughtsHub is a platform to drive community engagements and acquire inputs from communities in challenges that can be addressed and worked on during hackathons, in the line of enhancing service delivery and social cohesion.

Help shape South African Hackathons. How can technology help your community? Give local techies a direction on what to build for your community.
